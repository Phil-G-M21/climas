How it behaves

Two scenarios. One device.

CLIMAS reacts differently depending on context , because not every gas reading means the same thing.

01
Danger detected

The forgotten knob.

Gas concentration climbs while nobody's cooking. CLIMAS triggers: automatic phone call to your emergency contact, buzzer sounds, red LED flashes, OLED screen displays DANGER. All within seconds.

02
Override active

Cooking with the burner.

You press the override button before cooking. CLIMAS continues monitoring but won't alarm on expected spikes. When readings normalize, it re-arms automatically.

What it does

Six things CLIMAS handles for you.

01 / Call

Emergency phone call

When gas levels spike, CLIMAS automatically places a phone call to your emergency contact. No app required on their end.

02 / Watch

Climate monitoring

Temperature and humidity tracked continuously. The OLED display shows live readings at a glance, day or night.

03 / React

Instant alarm

Active piezo buzzer paired with a high-visibility red LED. Impossible to ignore when it matters.

04 / Defer

Cooking override

One button press suspends alerts while you cook. The device keeps monitoring and re-arms automatically once readings normalize.

05 / Display

OLED readout

Monochrome screen shows temperature, humidity, gas status, and DANGER warnings. Legible from across any room.

06 / Account

Live dashboard

Pro subscribers get a web portal with live readings, 24-hour charts, outdoor weather context, and a full alert history.
